Social And Business Entrepreneurs


There are two types of entrepreneurs, social and business? What makes a social entrepreneur a social entrepreneur. Why is he not a business one? Well, social entrepreneurs raise money for a cause like global warming. However business entrepreneurs usually make money for themselves and want to be their own boss.

Social entrepreneurship is an approach by individuals, groups, start-up companies or entrepreneurs, in which they develop, fund and implement solutions to social, cultural, or environmental issues. This concept may be applied to a wide range of organizations, which vary in size, aims, and beliefs.


An example of a social entrepreneur is Scott Harrison.




Social Entrepreneurs are:

  • Empathetic
  • Innovative
  • Socially Aware
  • Caring
